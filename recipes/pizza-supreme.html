Pizza Supreme

Home

Your next pizza night could start with the opening of the oven door instead of the front door. This pizza supreme recipe is a worthy match for anything you could order. The dough for the crust is so easy to prepare, and then you only need a few standard ingredients for the sauce and toppings.

What's on a supreme pizza? This recipe sets you up with the fundamental flavors but you can really customize it according to your taste. Since you're making it yourself, you can even top different sections with meats or vegetables to suit everyone.

Ingredients

Dough:

  • Active dry yeast
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt

Sauce:

  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Toppings:

  • Mozarella cheese
  • Canadian bacon
  • Pepperoni
  • Red pepper
  • Green pepper
  • Olives
  • Onion
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough: In a large bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water. Add the oil, salt, and a little flour. Using a food mixer, beat on medium speed for 3 minutes.
  2. Knead and rest: Stir in enough remaining flour to form a soft dough. Turn onto a floured surface and knead by hand for 6 to 8 minutes until smooth and elastic. Place in a greased bowl, cover, and let rest in a warm place for 10 minutes.
  3. Prepare sauce and bases: Combine the tomato sauce, onion, oregano, salt, and pepper; set aside. Divide the dough in half and roll each into a circle. Transfer to greased pizza pans and crimp the edges to raise them.
  4. Par-bake the crust: Bake the dough at 375°F for 15 minutes or until lightly browned.
  5. Add toppings and finish: Spread the sauce evenly over the crusts and sprinkle with your chosen toppings. Bake for an additional 15-20 minutes or until the cheese has melted.
